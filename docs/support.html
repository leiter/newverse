Frequently Asked Questions

General

Q: What is Newverse?

Newverse is a marketplace app that connects customers with local vendors and regional products in their area.

Q: Is Newverse free to use?

Yes, Newverse is free to download and use for customers.

Q: What platforms is Newverse available on?

Newverse is available on iOS (App Store) and Android (Google Play Store).

For Customers

Q: How do I place an order?

Browse products from local vendors, add items to your basket, and checkout. You'll receive confirmation with pickup details.

Q: When can I pick up my order?

Pickup is scheduled for Thursdays. Orders placed by Tuesday at 23:59 can be picked up the same week. Orders after that will be scheduled for the following Thursday.

Q: Can I modify my order after placing it?

Yes, you can edit your order until Tuesday at 23:59 before your scheduled pickup day.

For Sellers

Q: How do I become a seller on Newverse?

Contact us at the email below to learn about becoming a vendor on Newverse.

Q: How do I add products to my inventory?

Use the "Create Product" feature in the seller app to add new products with photos, descriptions, and pricing.

Delete Account

To delete your account and associated data:

  1. Open the app
  2. Go to Profile Settings
  3. Select "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm your choice

Or contact us at markro77@arcor.de to request account deletion.
